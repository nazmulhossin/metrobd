Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Metro Rail BD QR Ticketing System.

How do I generate a QR ticket?
To generate a QR ticket, go to the QR Tickets section and click on "Generate New Ticket". Select your starting and destination stations, choose the date and time, and complete the payment. Your QR ticket will be generated instantly.
How long is my QR ticket valid?
QR tickets are valid for the entire day of purchase until 11:59 PM. You can use the ticket anytime during this period for a single journey from the starting station to the destination station.
Can I cancel my QR ticket and get a refund?
Yes, you can cancel your QR ticket and get a refund if the ticket has not been used. Go to the QR Tickets section, find the ticket you want to cancel, and click on the "Cancel" button. The refund will be processed to your wallet balance within 24 hours.
How do I add balance to my wallet?
To add balance to your wallet, go to the Dashboard and click on "Add Balance" in the Quick Actions section. Select your preferred payment method, enter the amount you want to add, and complete the payment. The balance will be instantly added to your wallet.
What should I do if my QR code doesn't scan at the gate?
If your QR code doesn't scan at the gate, make sure your screen brightness is at maximum and the QR code is clearly visible. If the issue persists, approach the station staff for assistance. They can verify your ticket and help you through the gate.
